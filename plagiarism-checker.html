What is Plagiarism?

Plagiarism is using someone else's work without proper attribution. It includes copying text, ideas, or creative work without permission or credit.

Why Check for Plagiarism?

Plagiarism can lead to academic penalties, legal issues, and damage to your reputation. Always ensure your work is original.

Best Practices

Always cite sources, use quotation marks for direct quotes, and paraphrase properly while maintaining the original meaning.
