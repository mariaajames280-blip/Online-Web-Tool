1. Overview

Online ToolStack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website and tools.

Key Privacy Principles

  • Client-Side Processing: Most of our tools process data entirely in your browser
  • No Data Storage: We don't store your input data on our servers
  • Minimal Collection: We only collect essential information for website functionality
  • Transparency: We're clear about what data we collect and why

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Contact Forms: Name, email address, and message content when you contact us
  • Tool Inputs: Data you enter into our tools (processed client-side only)
  • Feedback: Comments, suggestions, or bug reports you submit

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: Pages visited, time spent, and interaction patterns
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes (anonymized)
  • Cookies: For website functionality and user preferences

2.3 Information We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Your tool input data (stays in your browser)
  • ❌ Personal files you process
  • ❌ Passwords or sensitive credentials
  • ❌ Financial or payment information
  • ❌ Social media profiles or connections

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Legitimate Purposes

  • Website Operation: Providing and maintaining our tools and services
  • Customer Support: Responding to your inquiries and providing assistance
  • Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Security: Protecting against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: Meeting legal obligations and protecting our rights

3.2 Marketing Communications

We do not send marketing emails or promotional content unless you explicitly opt-in. You can unsubscribe at any time.

4. Data Security

4.1 Technical Safeguards

  • HTTPS Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Client-Side Processing: Tool data processed locally in your browser
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard security measures for our servers
  • Access Controls: Restricted access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

4.2 Data Retention

  • Tool Data: Not stored on our servers (remains in your browser)
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized and retained for 2 years
  • Contact Information: Retained for 3 years or until you request deletion
  • Cookies: Expire according to their individual settings

5. Cookies and Tracking

5.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for website functionality, user preferences, and security. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how visitors use our website to improve user experience.

Advertising Cookies

Used by Google AdSense to display relevant advertisements.

5.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Services We Use

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics (anonymized)
  • Google AdSense: Displaying relevant advertisements
  • CDN Services: Faster content delivery (fonts, libraries)

6.2 Third-Party Privacy Policies

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense Privacy

7. Your Rights

7.1 GDPR Rights (EU Users)

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing

7.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@onlinetoolstack.com with a clear description of your request.

8. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Post the updated policy on this page
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Notify users of significant changes via our website
  • Maintain previous versions for reference
